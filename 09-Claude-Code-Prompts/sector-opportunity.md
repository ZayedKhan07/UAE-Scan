# Sector Opportunity Prompts

Reusable prompts for analyzing business opportunities across UAE sectors.

---

## Sector Deep Dive

```
Conduct a deep dive on the [SECTOR] in the UAE.

Cover:
- Market size and growth rate
- Key sub-segments and their dynamics
- Major players and market share
- Regulatory environment
- Entry barriers and capital requirements
- Realistic revenue potential for new entrants

Focus on actionable insights, not general overviews.
```

---

## Problem-Worth-Solving Analysis

```
Identify problems worth solving in UAE's [SECTOR].

For each problem:
- Describe the pain point clearly
- Who experiences this problem (be specific)
- How they currently solve it (or don't)
- Willingness and ability to pay
- Why existing solutions fail

Rank problems by opportunity size and execution feasibility.
```

---

## Target Customer Profiling

```
Profile the ideal target customer for [PRODUCT/SERVICE] in the UAE.

Include:
- Demographics and psychographics
- Where they are located (emirate, area)
- How they currently spend on this category
- Decision-making process and triggers
- Channels to reach them effectively
- Price sensitivity and expectations

Be specific enough to guide sales and marketing.
```

---

## Monetization Model Evaluation

```
Evaluate monetization models for [BUSINESS IDEA] in the UAE market.

Analyze:
- Subscription vs one-time payment viability
- B2B vs B2C pricing dynamics
- Typical price points in this category
- Payment behavior and preferences
- Revenue predictability and cash flow
- Upsell and expansion opportunities

Recommend the most viable model with reasoning.
```

---

## Execution Difficulty Assessment

```
Assess the execution difficulty of [BUSINESS IDEA] in the UAE.

Evaluate:
- Technical complexity
- Regulatory and licensing hurdles
- Capital requirements (startup and runway)
- Team and talent needs
- Time to first revenue
- Operational complexity at scale

Score each factor and provide overall difficulty rating (1-10).
```

---

## Risk and Limitation Mapping

```
Map the risks and limitations of pursuing [OPPORTUNITY] in the UAE.

Categories to cover:
- Market risks (demand, competition, timing)
- Regulatory risks (compliance, policy changes)
- Operational risks (talent, costs, logistics)
- Financial risks (capital, cash flow, margins)
- External risks (economic, geopolitical)

For each risk: likelihood, impact, and mitigation options.
```

---

## Opportunity Scoring

```
Score [BUSINESS OPPORTUNITY] against UAE Scan criteria.

Rate 1-10 on:
- Market demand evidence
- Revenue potential
- Execution feasibility
- Regulatory complexity
- Capital efficiency
- Competitive moat potential
- Alignment with available resources

Provide total score and go/no-go recommendation.
```
