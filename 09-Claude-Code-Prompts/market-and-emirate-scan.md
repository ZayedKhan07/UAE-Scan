# Market and Emirate Scan Prompts

Reusable prompts for scanning UAE market conditions and emirate-specific insights.

---

## General UAE Market Scan

```
Scan the current UAE market for [SECTOR/TOPIC].

Focus on:
- Current market size and growth trajectory
- Key players and competitive landscape
- Consumer/business demand signals
- Recent regulatory or policy changes
- Opportunities vs saturated segments

Output a structured summary with sources where possible.
```

---

## Emirate-Specific Analysis

```
Analyze [EMIRATE] as a location for [BUSINESS TYPE/ACTIVITY].

Cover:
- Economic profile and key industries
- Cost of operating (rent, labor, utilities)
- Licensing and freezone options available
- Target customer access and demographics
- Competitive density in this emirate
- Pros and cons vs other emirates

Provide a recommendation on emirate fit.
```

---

## Demand Signal Detection

```
Identify real demand signals in the UAE for [PRODUCT/SERVICE].

Look for:
- Search trends and online behavior
- Social media discussions and complaints
- Gaps in current market offerings
- Price sensitivity indicators
- Who is currently paying for alternatives

Separate hype from genuine demand.
```

---

## Competitive Landscape Scan

```
Map the competitive landscape for [INDUSTRY/NICHE] in the UAE.

Include:
- Major players and their market positioning
- Pricing tiers and business models
- Strengths and weaknesses of top competitors
- Underserved segments or customer complaints
- Barriers to entry for new players

Highlight where differentiation is possible.
```

---

## Trend vs Reality Check

```
Evaluate whether [TREND/OPPORTUNITY] in the UAE is real or overhyped.

Analyze:
- Evidence of actual adoption or spending
- Who is promoting this trend and why
- Comparable markets and their outcomes
- Regulatory readiness
- Execution barriers often overlooked

Give a clear verdict: real opportunity, wait-and-see, or avoid.
```

---

## Emirate Comparison Matrix

```
Compare [EMIRATE 1], [EMIRATE 2], and [EMIRATE 3] for [BUSINESS PURPOSE].

Create a comparison matrix covering:
- Setup costs and licensing fees
- Operational costs (rent, visas, utilities)
- Customer access and market size
- Regulatory environment
- Quality of life factors
- Strategic advantages

Recommend the best fit with reasoning.
```
