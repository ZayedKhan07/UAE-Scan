# CCM Go-To-Market Prompts

Reusable prompts for CCM App strategy, positioning, and market entry.

---

## Value Proposition Refinement

```
Refine the value proposition for CCM App targeting [CUSTOMER SEGMENT] in the UAE.

Address:
- Primary pain point being solved
- Quantifiable value delivered
- Differentiation from alternatives
- Why now (urgency/timing)
- Proof points and credibility builders

Output a clear, compelling value statement.
```

---

## Target Customer Prioritization

```
Prioritize target customer segments for CCM App in the UAE.

For each segment evaluate:
- Size of segment (number of potential customers)
- Urgency of the problem
- Ability to pay
- Accessibility (can we reach them)
- Sales cycle complexity
- Reference value for other segments

Rank segments and recommend initial focus.
```

---

## Pricing Strategy Analysis

```
Analyze pricing strategy options for CCM App in UAE market.

Consider:
- Competitor pricing benchmarks
- Customer willingness to pay
- Value-based vs cost-based pricing
- Pricing tiers and packaging options
- Free trial or freemium viability
- Enterprise vs SMB pricing differences

Recommend pricing structure with justification.
```

---

## Regulatory Fit Assessment

```
Assess CCM App's regulatory fit for [UAE CONTEXT/SECTOR].

Evaluate:
- Relevant regulations and compliance requirements
- How CCM App addresses regulatory needs
- Gaps or features needed for compliance
- Regulatory positioning as selling point
- Risk of regulatory changes
- Certification or approval requirements

Identify regulatory advantages to leverage in sales.
```

---

## Pilot Program Design

```
Design a pilot program for CCM App with [CUSTOMER TYPE].

Define:
- Pilot objectives and success metrics
- Ideal pilot customer profile
- Pilot scope and duration
- Resources required
- Risk mitigation approach
- Conversion path from pilot to paid
- Learning goals beyond revenue

Create actionable pilot framework.
```

---

## Sales Channel Strategy

```
Evaluate sales channel options for CCM App in the UAE.

Analyze:
- Direct sales viability and cost
- Partner/reseller opportunities
- Digital/inbound channels
- Industry events and conferences
- Government procurement pathways
- Referral and word-of-mouth potential

Recommend channel mix with resource allocation.
```

---

## Competitive Positioning

```
Position CCM App against [COMPETITOR/ALTERNATIVE] in UAE market.

Create:
- Feature comparison matrix
- Positioning statement
- Key differentiators to emphasize
- Weaknesses to address or avoid
- Objection handling points
- Win themes for sales conversations

Output battle card for sales use.
```

---

## Expansion Roadmap

```
Create expansion roadmap for CCM App beyond initial UAE market.

Consider:
- Geographic expansion sequence
- Vertical/industry expansion
- Product feature expansion
- Partnership expansion
- Team and capability expansion

For each phase: triggers, requirements, and risks.
```
