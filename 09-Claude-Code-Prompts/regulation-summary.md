# Regulation Summary Prompts

Reusable prompts for understanding UAE regulatory requirements and compliance.

---

## Regulatory Landscape Overview

```
Provide regulatory landscape overview for [INDUSTRY/ACTIVITY] in the UAE.

Cover:
- Primary regulatory bodies
- Key laws and regulations
- Licensing requirements
- Compliance obligations
- Recent regulatory changes
- Upcoming regulatory changes

Focus on practical implications for business operations.
```

---

## License Requirement Check

```
Determine licensing requirements for [BUSINESS ACTIVITY] in [LOCATION].

Identify:
- Type of license needed
- Issuing authority
- Eligibility requirements
- Application process and timeline
- Costs (initial and ongoing)
- Restrictions and conditions

Clarify freezone vs mainland differences if applicable.
```

---

## Compliance Checklist Generator

```
Generate compliance checklist for [BUSINESS TYPE] operating in UAE.

Categories:
- Corporate compliance (registration, filings)
- Tax compliance (corporate tax, VAT)
- Employment compliance (labor law, WPS)
- Industry-specific compliance
- Data protection and privacy
- Anti-money laundering

For each item: requirement, frequency, penalty for non-compliance.
```

---

## Freezone vs Mainland Comparison

```
Compare freezone vs mainland setup for [BUSINESS ACTIVITY].

Evaluate:
- Ownership structure differences
- License types available
- Cost comparison (setup and ongoing)
- Operational restrictions
- Customer access limitations
- Tax implications
- Visa allocations

Recommend best option with reasoning.
```

## Sector-Specific Regulation Deep Dive

```
Deep dive into regulations for [SPECIFIC SECTOR] in UAE.

Cover:
- Sector regulator and their mandate
- Licensing categories and requirements
- Operational regulations
- Reporting and audit requirements
- Penalties and enforcement approach
- Common compliance failures

Highlight what catches new entrants off guard.
```

## Tax Obligation Summary

```
Summarize tax obligations for [BUSINESS TYPE] in UAE.

Include:
- Corporate tax applicability and rates
- VAT registration and compliance
- Withholding tax considerations
- Transfer pricing rules (if applicable)
- Tax filing deadlines
- Record-keeping requirements
- Penalties for non-compliance

Clarify freezone tax benefits if applicable.
```

## Employment Law Essentials

```
Outline employment law essentials for hiring in UAE.

Cover:
- Employment contract requirements
- Visa and work permit process
- Wage Protection System (WPS)
- Leave entitlements
- End of service benefits
- Termination procedures
- Emiratization requirements (if applicable)

```

## Regulatory Risk Assessment

```
Assess regulatory risks for [BUSINESS MODEL] in UAE.

Evaluate:
- Current regulatory clarity

- Enforcement intensity
- Compliance cost burden

## Cross-Emirate Regulatory Comparison

```
Compare regulatory environment across emirates for [ACTIVITY].

For each relevant emirate:
- Regulatory authority
- Specific requirements
- Costs and fees
- Processing times
- Enforcement approach
- Notable differences

Identify most favorable regulatory environment.
