# Investment Analysis Prompts

Reusable prompts for evaluating investments and ROI in the UAE.

---

## Investment Opportunity Evaluation

```
Evaluate [INVESTMENT OPPORTUNITY] in UAE.

Analyze:
- Investment thesis and rationale
- Capital required and structure
- Expected returns (realistic range)
- Time horizon to returns
- Risk factors and probability
- Liquidity and exit options
- Comparison to alternatives

Provide go/no-go recommendation with confidence level.
```

---

## Real Estate Investment Analysis

```
Analyze real estate investment: [PROPERTY TYPE] in [LOCATION].

Cover:
- Purchase price and total acquisition cost
- Rental yield calculation (gross and net)
- Capital appreciation potential
- Operating costs and management
- Financing options and impact on returns
- Market supply/demand dynamics
- Risks specific to this investment

Compare to benchmark returns for UAE property.
```

---

## ROI Calculation Framework

```
Calculate ROI for [INVESTMENT/BUSINESS] in UAE.

Inputs needed:
- Initial investment: [AMOUNT]
- Ongoing costs: [MONTHLY/ANNUAL]
- Revenue projections: [ESTIMATES]
- Time horizon: [PERIOD]

Calculate:
- Gross ROI
- Net ROI (after all costs)
- Payback period
- IRR if applicable
- Break-even point

Sensitivity analysis on key assumptions.
```

## Cost-Benefit Analysis

```
Conduct cost-benefit analysis for [DECISION/INVESTMENT].

Costs to include:
- Direct costs (list all)
- Indirect costs (opportunity cost, time)
- Hidden costs (often overlooked items)
- Ongoing costs over [TIME PERIOD]

Benefits to include:
- Direct financial benefits
- Indirect benefits (strategic value)
- Risk reduction benefits
- Optionality created

Net present value and recommendation.
```

## Business Startup Cost Estimation

```
Estimate startup costs for [BUSINESS TYPE] in [LOCATION].

Categories:
- License and registration fees
- Office/premises costs
- Equipment and technology
- Initial inventory (if applicable)
- Marketing and launch costs
- Working capital buffer
- Visa and employment costs
- Professional fees (legal, accounting)
- Contingency

Total with monthly burn rate projection.
```

Fixed costs:

Variable costs:
- Cost of goods sold
- Marketing spend
- Transaction fees
- Travel and logistics

## Scenario Analysis

```
Conduct scenario analysis for [INVESTMENT/BUSINESS].

Model three scenarios:
- Optimistic: [DEFINE ASSUMPTIONS]
- Base case: [DEFINE ASSUMPTIONS]
- Pessimistic: [DEFINE ASSUMPTIONS]

For each scenario:
- Revenue projection
- Cost projection
- Profit/loss
- Cash position over time
- Key metrics

Identify scenario triggers and decision points.
```

## Passive Income Opportunity Scan

```
Scan passive income opportunities available in UAE with [CAPITAL AMOUNT].

Evaluate:
- Real estate rental yields
- Dividend-paying investments
- Fixed income options
- Business investments (silent partner)
- Digital asset opportunities

For each: expected return, risk level, liquidity, tax treatment.

Rank by risk-adjusted return.
```

## Investment Comparison Matrix

```
Compare investment options for [CAPITAL AMOUNT] in UAE.

Options to compare:
- [OPTION 1]
- [OPTION 2]
- [OPTION 3]

Criteria:
- Expected annual return
- Risk level (1-10)
- Liquidity
- Time commitment required
- Tax efficiency
- Downside protection

Create weighted scoring matrix and recommend allocation.
```

## Exit Strategy Analysis

```
Analyze exit strategies for [INVESTMENT/BUSINESS].

Evaluate exit options:
- Sale to strategic buyer
- Sale to financial buyer
- Management buyout
- Liquidation
- IPO (if applicable)

For each:
- Feasibility in UAE market
- Typical timeline
- Valuation implications
- Process and costs
- Tax considerations

Recommend primary and backup exit strategy.
```
